About Us

Burger House - is a chain of the best burgers in Montreal. We opened in 2018 and have already managed to win your hearts with quality products prepared with soul.

We are for quality! Only fresh meat, sauces and other ingredients. Only proven suppliers. And our homemade buns will not leave you indifferent.

Our 24/7 delivery service delivers hot burgers right to your doorstep. And monthly promotions cannot but rejoice. We take care of our clients as if they were our relatives.
